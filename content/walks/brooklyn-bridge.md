Walked the Brooklyn Bridge on a crisp Saturday morning. Started from the DUMBO side near the waterfront park and headed toward City Hall. The wooden boardwalk has so much character — you can feel the history under your feet.

The views of the Manhattan skyline from the midpoint are unreal. Stopped to grab coffee at a cart on the Manhattan side after crossing. Definitely the most iconic bridge walk so far.
